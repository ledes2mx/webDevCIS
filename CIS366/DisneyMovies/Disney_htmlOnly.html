Disney Movie Facts

  • 101 Dalmatians, Peter Pan, Lady and the Tramp, and Mulan are among the only animated Disney films where both parents are present and don't die during the course of the movie.
  • A hyena biologist sued Disney for portraying hyenas as villains in The Lion King.
  • Christian Bale voiced the character of Thomas in Pocahontas.
  • The group Abba was originally intended to do the music for The Lion King, but they weren't available, so Disney went with Elton John.
  • Frozen is the first animated Disney film directed by a woman.
  • Finding Nemo harmed clownfish populations after people started buying tons of pet clownfish after seeing the movie.
  • Andy didn't have a dad in Toy Story because human characters were expensive and difficult to animate.
  • It would take about 9.4 million balloons to lift Carl's house in Up.
  • Beauty and the Beast was the first ever animated film to be nominated for an Oscar for Best Film.
  • Pride Rock and the Gorge in The Lion King are modeled after Hell's Gate National Park in Kenya.
  • Tangled's Rapunzel and Flynn Rider make an appearance at Elsa's coronation in Frozen.
  • During the Snow White production, Walt Disney kept a menagerie of animals on the studio lot as live reference for the animators.
  • Walt Disney was presented with an honorary Oscar for Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, presented in one normal-sized statuette and seven miniature Oscars.
  • Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs was the first film to ever release a motion-picture soundtrack.
  • John Lennon turned down Walt Disney Studios' request for the Beatles to provide the voices of the vultures in The Jungle Book.
