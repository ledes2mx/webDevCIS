Tooltips

Tooltips can be attached to any element. When you hover the element with your mouse, the title attribute is displayed in a little box next to the element.

Any themes built with ThemeRoller will style tooltips accordingly, but the style can also be customized.

Tooltips are also useful for form elements, to show additional information for each field.

Hover over the field to see the tooltip.
